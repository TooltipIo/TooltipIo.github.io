FREQUENT QUESTIONS

DO MY USERS NEED TO INSTALL ANYTHING?

NO. Your users don't need to install anything to see your in-app messaging campaigns.

HOW LONG HAS TOOLTIP BEEN AROUND?

We started working on Tooltip in 2015, with the first version released in late summer. The product has been growing since, serving close to 200 customers.

WILL TOOLTIP SLOW DOWN MY SITE?

Tooltip will have almost no impact on performance of your site or web application. The size of the player file is around 73Kb. Comparable to Google Analytics, while providing more functionality.

HOW COME YOU'RE NOT SHOWING UP ON SIMILARWEB?

Due to the nature of our product, customers spend most of their time inside the Editor (Chrome extension). The bulk of our traffic is processed by our player script installed on customer sites to serve in-app messaging campaigns.

IS IT SECURE? RELIABLE?

VERY. Tooltip is built to handle large traffic on our customer sites and uses industry best practices in scaleable infrastructure and data management. We're ISO 27001 compliant and independently audited every year. Learn more here.

DO YOU HAVE A PUBLIC ROADMAP?

WE DO. Customer feedback is what drives new features we develop. We'd love to learn about your use cases to offer a Tooltip solution. See Public Roadmap.
